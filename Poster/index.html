Sonic Screwdriver Visualizations to make sense of High-Dimensional data

Since most visualizations are presented on a 2 dimensional plane, representing high-dimensional presents challenges for visualization designers. Capturing 3-dimensions on a 2d plane has some pretty well defined solutions, but presenting 4 or more requires some special tools - not unlike Dr. Who's Sonic Screwdriver.
